Scrambled Eggs with Cheese

by Aleaya Dixon

I make this breakfast any time I have a craving something savory in the morning because it is a delicious and cheesy breakfast. Who doesn't like cheese?

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 slices of any cheese of your choice
  • Bacon bits (optional)

What to do

  1. Crack eggs into a small or medium sized bowl
  2. Rip up cheese into small pieces and put into bowl with eggs
  3. Add bacon bits if you choose
  4. Use fork or wisk everything together
  5. Spray cooking spray into a medium sized pan then poor egg mixture into pan
  6. With the pan on the stove, turn the stove on a medium-high setting
  7. Use a spatula to stir and scrape the eggs in the pan until they are cooked just how you like them
  8. Plate your eggs and enjoy!
